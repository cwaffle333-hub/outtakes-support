Frequently Asked Questions

Outtakes is a movie ranking app where you log films you've watched and rank them through head-to-head battles. Instead of assigning star ratings, you compare movies directly — "Which was better?" — and the app builds your personal ranking from your choices. See how your taste stacks up against friends and the community.

When you log a movie, you'll go through a short series of head-to-head comparisons against movies you've already ranked. The app uses a binary search algorithm to find exactly where the new movie fits in your list — typically in just 4-6 comparisons. Your rating (out of 10) is then calculated from your movie's position in your personal ranking.

Download Outtakes from the App Store, open the app, and follow the sign-up flow. You'll choose a username, pick your favorite genres, and rank a few starter movies to calibrate your taste profile. The whole setup takes about 2 minutes.
